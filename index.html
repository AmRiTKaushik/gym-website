Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Is there a free trial before I commit to a membership?
Yes! We offer a complimentary 3-day trial pass for all new visitors. You'll get full access to the gym floor, one group class, and a brief consultation with one of our trainers — absolutely free with no commitment required.
What are the gym opening hours?
Basic members enjoy access from 6 AM to 10 PM, Standard members from 5 AM to 11 PM, and Premium members get 24/7 access year-round including public holidays. Staff are present during peak hours for all plans.
Do I need experience to join APEX GYM?
Absolutely not. APEX welcomes complete beginners. Every new member gets an orientation session where our trainers walk you through the equipment, assess your current fitness level, and outline a starting program that's right for you.
Can I pause or cancel my membership?
Yes. We offer flexible membership management. You can pause your plan for up to 2 months per year (for travel, health, etc.) or cancel with 15 days' notice before your next billing cycle. No cancellation fees — we believe in earning your loyalty.
What should I bring for my first workout?
Bring comfortable workout clothes, clean indoor training shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. We have locker rooms with showers and secure storage. Locks can be rented at the front desk for ₹10/day or purchased in our pro shop.
How do Personal Training sessions work?
PT sessions are 60 minutes each, conducted one-on-one with your assigned trainer. Sessions are fully customized based on your goals, fitness level, and progress. Standard plans include 4 sessions/month and Premium plans include 12 sessions/month. Additional sessions can be purchased at ₹500 each.
